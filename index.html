Steps For Baking a Cake

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups all purpose flour
  2. 2 teaspoons baking powder
  3. 1 teaspoon salt
  4. 1 cup unsalted butter
  5. 2 cups sugar
  6. 4 large eggs
  7. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. 1 cup whole milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Combine dry ingredients
  3. Cream Butter and Sugar
  4. Add Eggs
  5. Add Vanilla
  6. Alternate dry ingredients and Milk
  7. Pour Batter into pans
  8. Bake
  9. Cool
  10. Frosting

Now your cake is ready
